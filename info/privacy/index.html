Info Privacy Policy

Last updated: 30 June 2026.

Info is operated by BoohiCorp Enterprises Oy, Helsinki, Finland. Contact: otto.maenpaa@gmail.com.

Scope

This policy covers the Info iOS app and product pages. Info is a news intelligence app that uses GDELT-derived public media signals, source links, saved-topic preferences, and optional account features.

Data We Collect

  • Account data: email address, user identifier, display name, sign-in state, and authentication metadata through Supabase Auth.
  • User-owned content: saved topics, watchlists, bookmarks, read state, notes, notification rules, export requests, and deletion requests.
  • Purchase data: Info Plus subscription entitlement and renewal state through StoreKit and the App Store.
  • Public news context: GDELT-derived story signals, source URLs, entities, locations, timeline entries, and media-source metadata.
  • Support data: messages and request metadata when you contact support.

Data Not Collected

Info does not collect precise device location, contacts, health data, fitness data, financial information, advertising data, photos, videos, audio recordings, text messages, or cross-app tracking data. Map views use story locations from GDELT and app-owned backend tables, not device location permission.

How We Use Data

  • Create and manage accounts.
  • Sync saved topics, watchlists, bookmarks, notes, and read state.
  • Provide account export and account deletion workflows.
  • Restore and validate Info Plus subscription access.
  • Show story timelines, evidence cards, maps, and source-backed context.
  • Maintain security, prevent abuse, and debug user-reported issues.

Data Sharing

Info uses Supabase Auth, Supabase Postgres, Supabase Edge Functions, Apple StoreKit and App Store infrastructure, GDELT, and BigQuery-backed ingestion. The iOS app does not call BigQuery directly. Info does not sell personal data and does not share user data with advertising networks for tracking.

Retention And Deletion

Account data and user-owned content are retained while the account remains active or while retention is needed for security, fraud prevention, legal, support, or operational reasons. Signed-in users can request export or deletion from Settings. Deleting an Info account does not cancel Apple subscriptions; subscriptions are managed through Apple.

Your Choices

  • Use guest read-only mode for public news exploration when available.
  • Sign out from Settings.
  • Export app-owned account data from Settings after signing in.
  • Delete the Info account from Settings after signing in.
  • Manage or cancel Info Plus subscriptions through Apple.

Children

Info is not designed for children under 13.

Changes

We will update this page and the date above when practices change.
