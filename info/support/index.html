Privacy Requests

Signed-in users should start privacy requests from Settings using Export my data or Delete Account. If you cannot access your account, contact support and we will define a verification path before fulfilling privacy requests outside the app.

Delete Account

The Delete Account flow removes the Info account and app-owned rows after server-side validation. Deleting the Info account does not cancel Apple subscriptions.

Subscriptions

Info Plus subscriptions are managed by Apple. Users can restore purchases in Info and manage or cancel subscriptions through Apple subscription settings or the StoreKit manage subscription sheet when available. Refund and billing handling follows Apple's App Store processes.
