Info

News With Memory. Info helps you understand what changed, what evidence supports it, and what signals to watch next.

Info turns public media signals from GDELT and app-owned analysis into daily briefs, story timelines, evidence cards, source links, map context, and saved-topic watchlists.

Daily Brief

Finite context for stories that changed, without an endless feed.

Story Timelines

See how coverage, sources, entities, and locations evolve.

Evidence Cards

Inspect source-backed claims and provenance before reacting.

Map Context

Understand where a story is being reported and discussed.

Source And Provenance

Info uses GDELT-derived public media signals and source links to build context. GDELT coverage and app-generated insights are signals, not guarantees that a story is complete, neutral, or error-free. Important decisions should always be checked against original sources.

Info Plus

Info Plus is the planned subscription for deeper story memory, premium watchlists, evidence packs, and advanced alerts. Pricing and renewal details are provided by the App Store in the app.
